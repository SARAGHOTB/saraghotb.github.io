What makes painting a great hobby?

Physics is an art

If you are a physicist you probably know that nature is a pure art. The geometry in the nature, the micro-organisms, the life under the microscope, the magnetic lines all represent an original outstanding art which is inspiring.

It brings happiness

Art brings happiness. Even viewing art triggered a surge of dopamine into the brain, which results in feelings of pleasure and satisfaction. Now imagine if you create art yourself. The feeling is awesome.
