About me

I am currently a PhD candidate in condensed matter physics and I work with superconducting material in a cool laboratory. In my free time, I love to paint read. I like digital painting and also I have some experience in oil paining and drawing. I am also a bibliophile and I never get tired of reading. My taste in reading is pretty wide. I am mostly interested in 18th and 19th literature as well as graphic novels, self help books and poetry and basically any good book that bring me to a new world of imagination. It's been almost three years that I post review about the book that I read on Goodreads and I try to put my most favorite books in this page. On my free time I learnt a bit of HTML and CSS and I built this webpage with my basic knowledge on web development, I am learning every day and I will try to update and upgrade the page.
